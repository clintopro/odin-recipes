Ingridents

  • 2 cups of rice
  • Coconut milk
  • 3 bell pepper
  • 3 maggi cube
  • 1kg chicken
  • Pineapple
  • Salt
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Brown Sugar

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice several times until the water runs clear. Drain the rice.
  2. In a medium pot, over high heat, add the rice, broth, and coconut milk and bring to a boil. Once boiling, turn the heat to low and cover with tight fitting lid; let cook for 15 minutes.
  3. Fluff the rice with a fork and mix in the pineapple, soy sauce, sesame oil, brown sugar, and pepper
  4. Top with any of the other recommended toppings (below) and drizzle a touch more soy sauce, if desired.
