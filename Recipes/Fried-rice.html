Fried Rice

Description

. It is perfectly okay to call this recipe West African fried rice because we generally cook fried rice the same way across West Africa – Nigeria, Ghana, Cameroon, Togo, Cotonou, etc.

Ingridents

  • 2 cups of rice
  • butter
  • 3 bell pepper
  • 3 maggi cube
  • 1kg chicken
  • sautéed vegetables

Steps

  1. First, cook the rice in a well-seasoned broth.
  2. Second, fry the rice in sautéed vegetables.
